🛡️ Privacy Policy - Truth Buster AI

Last updated: July 23, 2026

Truth Buster AI ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our app handles your information.

1. Information We Collect

We do NOT collect, store, or share any personal data.

Truth Buster AI operates entirely on your device and through our hosted web service. We do not require you to create an account, provide your name, email, phone number, or any other personal information.

2. How the App Works

When you use Truth Buster AI:

  • Text you paste is analyzed in real-time and is not stored on any server after analysis.
  • URLs you provide are temporarily fetched to extract article content for analysis. We do not log or save these URLs.
  • Images you upload are processed locally for forensic analysis and are not uploaded to any external server.

3. Data Storage

We do not maintain any database of user queries, results, or personal information. Each analysis session is independent and temporary.

4. Third-Party Services

The app uses Streamlit Cloud for hosting. Streamlit's own privacy policy applies to their hosting infrastructure. No user data is shared with advertising networks, analytics providers, or any other third parties.

5. Permissions

The app requires the following permissions:

  • Internet Access — Required to load the web application and fetch article URLs for analysis.
  • File Access — Required only when you choose to upload an image for visual forensic analysis.

6. Children's Privacy

Truth Buster AI is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children or any other users.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our GitHub repository: github.com/Spoorti-I/truth-buster-ai
